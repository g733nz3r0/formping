// FAQ

Questions answered.

Do I need a backend or server? +
No. FormPing is entirely frontend. You add one script tag. We handle everything on our servers. No Node.js, PHP, or backend required whatsoever.
Which WhatsApp number do I use? +
Your personal or business WhatsApp number. Connect it in your FormPing dashboard in under 2 minutes. You receive pings exactly like regular WhatsApp messages.
What are the ads on the free plan? +
Small, non-intrusive Google Ads appear inside your FormPing dashboard — not on your website. Upgrade to Pro at any time to remove them completely.
Does it work with any form builder? +
Yes. HTML forms, React, Vue, WordPress, Webflow, Wix — any form with a standard submit event. If it can submit, FormPing can ping it.
What happens if I exceed my limit? +
Your form still works normally and submissions are saved in your dashboard. WhatsApp delivery pauses until the next billing cycle or until you upgrade.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. All data is SSL encrypted in transit. We never store card details — payments are handled entirely by Paystack on their PCI-compliant servers. We are GDPR compliant.
Can I get a refund? +
Yes. We offer a full 30-day money-back guarantee on Pro plans, no questions asked. Just email support@formping.io and your refund is processed within 48 hours.
